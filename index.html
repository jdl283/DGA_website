Welcome to the website of the Development in Games Association (DGA) at Cornell.

DGA seeks to foster a constructive learning environment for students interested in Game Development.

We host weekly “Dev Sessions” to explore various topics in game development such as art, programming, and design. NO prior experience is required to participate and everyone is welcome to attend.
